The Story

Salt Company exists to reach the next generation at Mizzou.

Since 2016, Salt Company has been gathering on Mizzou's campus — not as a club or a program, but as a movement. Every week, college students show up hungry to know Jesus, to be known in community, and to be sent out on mission. And God has been faithful.

Students who walked in skeptical have been baptized. Students who came in lonely have found a family. Students who had no direction have been sent to the nations. What started as a handful of gatherings has become a vibrant college ministry seeing Luke 10:02 come to life — and it's only the beginning.

Salt Company is the college ministry of Anthem Church in Columbia, Missouri — part of the Salt Network of churches committed to reaching the next generation. Everything we do is fueled by a local church that believes the gospel is for everyone — including the 30,000+ students walking Mizzou's campus every day.

Where We're Going

We have a bold vision. And we want you to be part of it.

Anthem Church is stepping into the biggest season in our history. We're calling it For Good — a two-year initiative to expand our ministry, strengthen our mission, and build a permanent home that positions us to reach Columbia and Mizzou's campus for decades to come.

"God is doing something significant in our church — something far bigger than a building, a campaign, or a moment. He is forming us into a people who build, pray, serve, give, and love for good."

This vision directly fuels Salt Company — expanded spaces for worship and discipleship, deeper investment in campus ministry, greater resources to reach students, and a foundation that ensures this work doesn't just last a semester, but for generations.

We're hosting a small number of gatherings to share the full vision with the people who love what God is doing through Salt Company. If you're a parent, an alum, or a friend of this ministry — we'd love for you to be in the room.
